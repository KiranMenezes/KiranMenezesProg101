Information on both species of Pandas; Giant and Red


Giant Panda - it's not a BEAR!

Giant Pandas are found in thick bamboo forests high in the moutains of central China.


Red Panda - Smaller than Giant Panda.

The smaller of the Panda species know as the Red Panda, inhabit the same high-altitude bamboo forests in the mountain of central China.
