Giant Panda Bear

Basic Facts

Giant Pandas live in the high-altitude mountain forests in central China. Even though Giant Panda are known to most as herbivours, Pandas have been known to prey on birds and small rodents.





These furry beasts live on a diet which primarily consists of Bamboo. The Giant Panda can consume an average of 28 pounds of Bamboo on a daily basis.
