Giant Pandas Habitat

Environmetal Conditions



The Giant Panda exists mainly in Sichuan, but also in neighbouring Shaanxi and Gansu. As a result of farming, deforestation, and other development, the giant panda has been driven out of the lowland areas where it once lived.





Wild Pandas that inhabit the high-altitude forests live in cold and wet environment. Luckily however, the Panda is well equipped to survive in this type of environment.



Pandas have been seen at elevations from ten thousand feet to thirteen thousand feet high in the mountains.

The cuddly looking bears are often obsereved eating, sitting down on their haunches, in a relaxed sort of way, with their hind legs stretched out before them.



Even though Pandas seem to be lazy lumbering bears, they can swim with great ease and climb trees efficiently.
