Red Panda Bear

Basic Facts

The Red Panda inhabits the same high-altitude mountain forests as their bigger counsin, the Giant Panda. Each Red Panda when fully grown is close to the size of your typical house cat.




A distict characteristic of these tree climbing bears, are their furry ringed tails. In the cold climate their tail keeps them warm when they sleep at night.
